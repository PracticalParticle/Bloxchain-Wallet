# Professional Standards (Code of Conduct)

## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and productive environment for Blox-Apps: an application hub for Bloxchain-based applications. We focus on technical excellence, security, and clear collaboration between official and community contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- **Respectful communication** in all technical discussions and interactions  
- **Constructive feedback** focused on code quality and technical merit  
- **Professional collaboration** when contributing to official apps or community blox  
- **Respect for intellectual property and licensing** (Official-Blox vs community-blox; see [contracts/community-blox/README.md](contracts/community-blox/README.md))  
- **Security-first mindset** in contributions and discussions  

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not tolerated:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks  
- Discriminatory language or behavior  
- Publishing others’ private information without permission  
- Threats or intimidation  
- Spam, excessive self-promotion, or off-topic disruption  

## Scope

These standards apply to all Blox-Apps community interactions, including:

- Contributing to Official-Blox or adding community blox  
- Code reviews and technical discussions  
- Documentation and pull requests  
- Any official project communication  

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Report violations to:

- **Email**: conduct@particlecs.com  
- **Subject**: `[CONDUCT] Blox-Apps – Professional Standards Report`  

Please include: description of the incident, date/time, location (e.g. GitHub, email), names if known, and your contact information.

### Process

1. We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours.  
2. We will investigate confidentially.  
3. We will take appropriate action and follow up with involved parties.  

Consequences may include a private warning, a public warning, a temporary ban, or a permanent ban from the Blox-Apps community.
